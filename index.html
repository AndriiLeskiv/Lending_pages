Banks and corporations are investing in quantum companies

Goldman Sachs and Citigroup in 2018 invested $6.5 million (468 million rubles at the current exchange rate) in the developer of quantum algorithms QC Ware.
Goldman Sachs has repeatedly backed the Canadian hardware maker D-Wave. In 2014, the amount of investments from Goldman Sachs (GS) and a number of other players amounted to 30 million Canadian dollars (1.8 billion rubles).

The number of startups developing products in the field of post-quantum cryptography is growing on the market, and scientists predict a multiple increase in demand for such solutions immediately after the standardization stage is completed.
